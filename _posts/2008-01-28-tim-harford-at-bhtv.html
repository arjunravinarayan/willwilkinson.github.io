--- layout: post title: Tim Harford at BHTV categories: - Bloggingheads - Economics tags: [] status: publish type: post published: true meta: {} --- Today on Free Will I chat with Tim Harford about his new book The Logic of Life. I wasn't expecting to discover work that would help me on my inequality project, but I did.
