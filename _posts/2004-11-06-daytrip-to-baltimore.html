--- layout: post title: Daytrip to Baltimore categories: - Whatever tags: [] status: publish type: post published: true meta: {} --- Back from a lovely day in Charm City. If you're a DC-ite, or visiting Baltimore from elsewher, The American Visionary Art Museum is well worth the trip. I can be pretty skeptical of "outsider art," and there are a few drunk, short-bus-riding pre-schooler paintings, but there was also a lot of really amazing stuff in the collection. It's small, so it can be enjoyed beginning-to-end in a couple hours. And then take a walk around Federal Hill and enjoy the view of the harbor and the Baltimore skyline. After, we found a great little coffe shop, Patterson Perk, across from Patterson Park. Also recommended.
